One platform.
Two sides of casting.

A single infrastructure connects talents and productions — from the first scroll to the final booking.

Talent · Mobile & Web Talent personalized casting feed — AI-matched roles, performance profile, and top castings near her. Sorted by fit score, not recency.
Production · Desktop Real-time casting dashboard for your productions — submissions, shortlisted, callbacks, booked. Every role tracked at a glance.
Production · Desktop Search engine for talents by profile and launch campaigns and casting calls for your projects — eg: "vulnerable, sharp, 24–32, drama-leaning" — and get results ranked by AI performance fit, not keyword.

Production · Desktop Collaborative review. Casting directors, producers, directors — same place, real-time ratings, threaded comments, shortlists, callbacks. AI scene analysis built in.

Our moat

We don't sell software.
We build a non-reproducible dataset of human performance.

Every audition on Let it Cast is structured the same way: same scenes, same prompts, same comparable conditions. Casting decisions — feedback, shortlists, outcomes — become labels. Over time the dataset compounds. It cannot be reconstructed retroactively.
